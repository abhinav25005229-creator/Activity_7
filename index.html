Smart Travel Tips

A few simple ideas to make your journey smoother, safer and more memorable.

  • 1. Keep documents in digital + physical form
    Always carry soft copies of passport, ID, tickets and hotel vouchers in your email / phone, along with one printed set.
  • 2. Travel light but smart
    Pack versatile clothes, a light jacket, basic medicines, and a universal adapter instead of stuffing your luggage.
  • 3. Respect local culture
    Learn a few basic phrases, follow dress codes (if any), and be mindful of local customs and photography rules.
  • 4. Keep some local currency handy
    Even if you use cards / UPI, small shops, taxis or local markets may prefer cash.
